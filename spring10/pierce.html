Programming Molecular Function

Niles Pierce, Caltech
March 11th, 2010 at 2PM–3PM in 740 Evans Hall [Map]

This talk will describe efficient algorithms for the analysis and design of synthetic nucleic acid devices. These computational tools facilitate the pursuit of programmable molecular technologies relevant to longstanding challenges in biological imaging and cancer treatment.
